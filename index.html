About Me
My Background
I received my bachelor's degree in psychology and went on to receive my master's degree in secondary education and math instruction. I have my administrative credential and have worked in administration for the past 10 years.
My Experience
I have worked in education for the past 20 years. I've been a substitute teacher, math teacher, math coach, district coach, assistant principal, and currently serve as a middle school principal. I am an educational consultant and an adjunct teacher at San Diego State University, where I teach aspiring administrators about visionary leadership.
Personal Life
